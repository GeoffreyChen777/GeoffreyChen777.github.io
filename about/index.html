RESEARCH INTERESTS AND STATEMENTS

My research interests focus on object detection and segmentation in many application environments (e.g., drone-captured images, underwater images). I have developed two underwater robots to deploy and test our algorithms for the real-world application. Besides, I also interest in mining the relation in natural images to boost the deep learning segmentation algorithm under weakly supervised or unsupervised condition.
